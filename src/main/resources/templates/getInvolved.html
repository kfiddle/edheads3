Get Involved

You can support Edheads in a wide variety of ways!

Donate to convert Flash games to HTML5, or to support the company.

Sponsor a page, an event or a section of content on our web site. Edheads reaches millions of students each year, making your sponsorship dollar go a long way!

Volunteer for Edheads! Help us meet our mission to interest a wide variety of students in STEM careers by volunteering to help with events, content, fundraising, administration or in other ways. Check out our volunteer listings on volunteermatch.org, when you get to the page, search under ‘Edheads’ to see all our listings.

Or, share your ideas of how to help Edheads meet our mission of steering K12 students toward STEM careers!

Email us at info@edheads.org to learn more.
